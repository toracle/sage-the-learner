Sage the Learner

A Seeker's Journey to Wisdom in Learning
"Accumulating knowledge does not accumulate wisdom. Wisdom grows from the way you learn."

The story of Sage, a young scholar who wanted to learn how to learn, and his pilgrimage to the Mountain of Contemplation to meet a sage. Through twenty episodes, the book explores the principles of metacognition, memory, motivation, collaboration, and reflection.

Insights from cognitive science and educational psychology, told as a narrative. As you follow Sage's journey, you will begin to draw your own map of learning.
